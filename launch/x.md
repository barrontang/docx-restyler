I just published **docx-restyler**.

It’s an OpenClaw skill prototype for a very practical workflow:
- take a source Word document
- take a reference template Word document
- output a new finalized `.docx` aligned to the template

I also tuned it for Chinese formal-document heading structures like:
- 一、
- （一）
- 1.
- （1）

Repo:
https://github.com/barrontang/docx-restyler
